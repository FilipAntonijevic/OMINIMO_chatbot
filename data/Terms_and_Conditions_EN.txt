7. AUTOMATED DECISION MAKING, PROFILE CREATION

The data subject has the right not to be covered by the scope of a decision based solely on automated data management, including profiling, which would have a legal effect on him or affect him to a similar extent. The Insurer is also entitled to make a decision based on the evaluation of the personal characteristics of the data subject exclusively through automated data management, if it is necessary for the conclusion or performance of the insurance contract between the data subject and the Insurer, or if the data subject has given his express consent.

Profiling is any form of automated processing of personal data in which personal data is used to evaluate certain personal characteristics of a natural person, in particular to analyze or predict characteristics related to work performance, economic situation, health, personal preferences, interests, reliability, behavior, location or movement.

In addition to functions based on artificial intelligence, the Insurer performs the verification and evaluation of the customer's image creation during customer screening by means of video identification. As part of this, it is verified that the Customer who appears as the subject of the screening at the remote location is a real, live person, that the online screening system is used personally in real time, and that the live image has not been manipulated. Machine functions suitable for the interpretation and evaluation of the content are used during the indirect electronic customer screening: document recognition, document detection, reading (MRZ, barcode), face detection, coding, comparison, sharpness test and vitality test data and evaluation, the results of the evaluation. During the customer due diligence procedure, the results of the test are evaluated based on the data provided by the customer with the help of machine functions. During the indirect electronic customer due diligence process, the Insurer compares the customer's photograph recorded with the image in the official identity card using the audited electronic communication device.
